American Apple Pie Recipe

Hunger quenching American Apple pie recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plain flour
  • 1/2 self-raising flour
  • 1/4 cup cornflour
  • 1/4 custard powder
  • 1 tbs caster sugar
  • 100g cold butter, chopped coarsely
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup iced water
  • 10 medium apples
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 caster sugar
  • 1 tsp finely grated lemon rind
  • 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 egg white, for brushing
  • 1 tbsp caster sugar, extra, for sprinkling
